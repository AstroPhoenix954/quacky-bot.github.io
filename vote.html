Vote for Quacky Bot

Voting for Quacky helps Show your Support for Quacky Bot! When you vote, you will get additional bot perks for showing your support.

Voting on discord.boats will get you the "Voted" Badge on your Profile for the next 12 hours.

Voting on top.gg will get you the "Voted" badge for 1.5 Days as long as you're in the Quacky Support Server.

By Voting, you also get access to the -stats command!
