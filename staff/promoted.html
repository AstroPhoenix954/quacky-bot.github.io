Quacky Bot Moderator Promotion

Congradulations! You've been Promoted to be a Moderator!

Your Job

As a moderator, your job is to find Chat Offenders, assist Helpers in Moderation, and Manage Reports.

Remember that as a Moderator you are representing the Quacky Bot Staff Team.

Your New Permissions

As a moderator, you have been granted the following new permissions:

  1. Access to the Message Logs.
  2. Permission to Kick Users.
  3. Permission to Mute Users.
  4. Permission to View User Reports.

Managing Reports and Support Tickets

As a moderator, you get access to Support Tickets and Reports.

Click Here to Read on how to Manage Support Tickets

To manage reports, you will need solid evidence to take moderation actions on a user.

If a report relates to an action in the chat you will need to use your judgement in order to take/not take moderation actions on the user.

If a report is related to DM Advertising ask for a screenshot of the user's mutual servers in order to prove that the message was not faked.

If the user cannot show the mutual servers mention an admin to take over the ticket. If they can, take moderation actions on the user.
