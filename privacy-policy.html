Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy for Quacky Bot

We do not store any detailed information about you.

However, the information that is stored is about how many bugs you've found and your donator rank (if applicable).

If at any time you become blacklisted from our bot, your information will be added to our blacklist.

What Rank Information is Stored?

When you get blacklisted your userid, the admins userid, the date, and reason are stored.

When you get donate your userid and the rank you have are stored.

When you get find an error with Quacky your userid and the number of bugs you've found are stored.

Why do we store this information?

This information is stored in order to provide you with badges and to notify the bot if you're allowed to run certain commands or not. This is manually added and is not automatic.
