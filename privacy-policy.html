Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy for Quacky Bot

What information is stored?

The information that Quacky stores is about how many bugs you've found and your donator rank (if applicable).

If at any time you become blacklisted from our bot, blacklist information will be stored.

When you get blacklisted your userid, the admins userid, the date, and reason are stored.

When you get donate your userid and the rank you have are stored.

When you get find an error with Quacky your userid and the number of bugs you've found are stored.

Why we store the information and how we use it.

This information is stored in order to provide you with badges and to notify the bot if you're allowed to run certain commands or not. This information is manually added to the bot.

Who gets this data?

Donator and error data can seen by anyone with the -badges command.

Other than people seeing you're blacklisted, only Admins have access to view detailed blacklist information.

Questions and Concerns.

If you are concerned about the data stored Join the Quacky Support Server or DM DuckMasterAl#0001 on Discord.

How to Remove your data.

If you would like to remove your data, you can Join the Quacky Support Server and type "!cleardata" in a commands channel.

If you are banned from the support server, DM DuckMasterAl#0001 on Discord to remove the data.

Clearing this data does not clear blacklist data.
